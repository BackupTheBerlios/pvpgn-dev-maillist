Comment:
On official servers, all members in a WAR3/W3XP clan with the rank Cheiftain or Shaman are given OPs in their clan channel automatically.  When I say Ops, I mean the yellow bar around their name with the "hammer" privileges (the ability to kick and ban players from the clan channel).

Note that the only way to do this now is give the person /tmpop and hope they don't disconnect.  /op and /aop and /admin do not work very well for this purpose, as most clans will use a bot for moderating the channel.  If the bot doesn't see itself with having the gold bar / hammer icon it won't try to use /kick or /ban.  /aop /admin and /op users do not get the yellow bar around their name.  /tmpop users do, however with clans this doesn't work too well because an admin has to give /tmpop to the cheiftain and shamans every time they join the channel (since its /tmpop they lose the yellow bar / hammer icon when they leave and don't get it when they come back.  In the official server all shamans and the cheiftain automatically get the hammer / yellow bar when they join their clan channel.)
