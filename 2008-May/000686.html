Summary: unable to join clan channel post game

Details: Minor, annoying bug.
Follow these steps to reproduce:
1. Join a clan channel (not your clan)
2. Bnetd will only let you in if you are a member of that clan, or if there is a clan member in that channel
3. Play a game
4. Leave game when finished

bnetd automatically rejoins your previous channel before removing you from the game. If there are no members of the clan in the channel anymore, your join will fail. Unfortunately, you are still marked as being in a game, which means some server commands are disabled (such as joining channels). i.e. you are now unable to do anything. To fix you have to disconnect from the server.

Here is a patch, made from the SVN 1.99.r446 (latest at time of writing)
"Fix" joins you to THE VOID if the bug is detected. Thereafter you can join whatever channel you want. There are many ways to fix this, this was just my take on it.

http://pastebin.com/f5999b902
