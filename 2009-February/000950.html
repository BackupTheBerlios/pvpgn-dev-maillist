"/clan whisper" doesn't behave properly. Instead of returning, it just filters down the command. For chieftains, this means nothing. For normal users, it means they get a message "You are not the chieftain of clan!", even though the message was sent.

The fix is just to return out the command, example in this commit:
http://github.com/marcbowes/pvpgn-twilight/commit/c10ba44b98f5612aa05a63d197ecf9c39f41430f
