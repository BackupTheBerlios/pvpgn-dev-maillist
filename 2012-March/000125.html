In D2/D2: LoD:

In SC/SC: BW
Announcement from <admin account>: <announcement text>

2. Watchlist / friendlist enter/leave messages
The same issue - they appear as a whisper from an account *PvPGN_Realm

The patch makes them appear as a system server message as the one above.

I believe leaving them the way they were, could be a player's risk of receiving a fake announcement from another player and get his account compromised. Also, leaving it as a whisper may at least result in a case where players don't really pay much attention to what it is said in it.

3. Fixed SC:BW client tag in server messages as on Bnet:
Used to be: StarCraft: BroodWars
Now: StarCraft: Brood War

The patch has been tested how it appears in the following clients:
STAR, SEXP, DRTL, D2DV, D2XP and works properly.

Note: It has NOT been tested with WAR3, W3XP and W2BN.
