What is a rollback:
A rollback causes the game to drop, without saving your character. What this means is you can make an Enigma, look at the stats, then roll it back to being a 3os armor with jah/ith/ber outside of it. You can also revert items back to being unid, and hundreds of other useful things.

The method:
Spam overhead messages while casting a missile spell (Fire Blast works well and only rq level 2, fire bolt should work as well).

The process:
There are many ways to do this. The best is probably this.

1) Download RedVex: http://bhfiles.com/files/Diablo%20II/1.12a/RedVex/Redvex_3.0_6-12-07.rar
2) Download ASCII Macro: http://bhfiles.com/files/Diablo%20II/1.12a/RedVex/Plugins/Ascii%20Macro%201.1.rar
3) Make a file, "crash.txt" in the Macros folder.
4) Fill that file with the following:
Code:

01oVERHEADdATAoVERFLOW OverheadDataOverflow OverheadDataOverflow oVERHEADdATAoVERFLOW

04oVERHEADdATAoVERFLOW OverheadDataOverflow OverheadDataOverflow oVERHEADdATAoVERFLOW

5) Get into game, leave town.
6) Start spamming fire blast, then type ".overhead crash"

If done properly, it should crash by the third message is sent, every time. This is the method that is being sold by a lot of people, including benowns (you might have seen him selling it in his signature). Despite Mibz being a retard and removing it, it DOES in fact work. If you use it right.
