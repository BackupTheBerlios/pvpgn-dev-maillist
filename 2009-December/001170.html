Summary: Update news only with /rehash

Follow-Ups:

Date: 2009-Dec-01 15:19
By: saidin

Comment:
This patch just improves /rehash by allowing for an argument (currently only "news"). This lets you rehash specific aspects only. This allows the news to be updated without reloading all the other config and all the other stuff that the restart does (such as rejoining people into channels).

This is meant to be used with an automated news updater (e.g. via RSS).
