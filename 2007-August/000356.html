I think that reconnection only worked when the connection to mysql
timed out NOT when you restart mysql. Just make sure you restart
pvpgn. In any case you should NEVER EVER stop mysql while pvpgn is
running, that's just asking for trouble (ie corruption of the account
database contents). Always shutdown pvpgn, THEN play with your mysql
server, then start pvpgn.
