Summary: Clan Rank Exploit

Details: Any user in a clan may promote/demote themselves/any other member of their clan to any other level by sending packets.

The following (illegal) packet may be used to accomplish this:

FF7A190001000000<null terminated-string Username (3-15 chars exluding Null)><WORD Rank Flag>

Rank Flag 01->04 (Peon, Grunt, Shaman, Chieftain).

Setting Rank Flag to 00 may remove a user from the clan, though I haven't tested it.
