Date: 2008-Feb-11 11:28
By: zzzoom

Comment:
bnetd currently replies to a gamelist request with the first few games in its gamelist that fit in a packet, which makes older open games not visible in high traffic servers.
This patch adds a single serverwide cursor to the gamelist so one gamelist starts where the last left off. This gives an even distribution of "air time" for all games.
