leave it up to the user on weather or not to use zlib with stdcall or
cdecl and have them define it in their zlib.h. This may not be the
preferred way but it fixed my problems and should help others
compiling on MinGW compilers. Even when your zlib is compiled with
stdcall you still get errors in that area when compiling. (which is
how it was originally defined) I have Included the patch as the
attachment.
