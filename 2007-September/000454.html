Summary: Diablo 2 Client String

Details: When connecting on Diablo II, expansion or original, the client string is send differently between using open or closed pvpgn.

By client string, I am referring to D2DV, D2XP, WAR3, W3XP, STAR, etc.

On Diablo II Closed, it is sent as this.
PX2D

On Diablo II Open, it is sent as this.
D2XP

This poses an error to many bots, as they are not checking for the stat string to be D2XP -- they are checking for PX2D.
